NetSeed Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2023

Welcome to NetSeed products and services!

NetSeed ("we," "our," or "us") is a product (service) that allows customers (individuals or organizations) to directly connect servers, computers, and mobile devices in a blockchain-based network, where each connection is encrypted.

NetSeed is committed to enhancing transparency in information processing. We utilize a decentralized blockchain account system to maximize the protection of your privacy and security. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose information through our website and any other websites or mobile applications linked to this Privacy Policy, as well as any other interactions you may have with us (such as customer support, events, our social media pages, and other communications) (collectively referred to as the "Services").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we handle your information. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use these Services.

Please note that this Privacy Policy applies to situations where NetSeed is the data controller of your information. This includes the information we collect from visitors to our website ("Website Visitors"), prospective users and customers ("Prospects"), and users of the NetSeed solution ("Users"), collectively referred to as "you" in this document.

Special Note: When you choose to use NetSeed with a decentralized blockchain server mode, all your data belongs solely to you, and no one else, including us, can access or modify your data.

In providing our services, we may also process certain information about Users on behalf of organizations ("Customers") who have contracted to use the NetSeed solution under the applicable master services agreement or terms of service. In such cases, our Customers are the data controllers, and we act as a "data processor" or "service provider" under applicable laws. This means that our Customers are responsible for handling information in accordance with their own privacy policies and practices and complying with applicable data protection laws. When we act as a data processor, we will only process information about Users of Customer accounts in accordance with the Customer's instructions and the applicable data processing agreement we have with the Customer, or as otherwise required by applicable law.
